Getting Started

Upon entering the survival server, you will join the spawn area, where you can find Seasonal Crates, Leaderboards, Shop, Tavern, and other NPCs to interact with!Survival is a friendly and peaceful server that does not include raiding or griefing in claimed land.

After exploring the spawn area, you can type /kit starter once to obtain some useful items to begin your survival journey. After claiming your starting items, you may type /wildto get teleported randomly in Overworld. To get back at spawn area, you can type /spawn.

Players can complete jobs for money that can be used to purchase items in player auctions and shops. Players can also utilize their time to complete The Battle pass quests for rewards. These rewards supply you with Bryxel Relics, which is one of the main currencies in Survival. You can use this relic to purchase items from /shop which lets you buy Server cosmetic items.



Tavern

The Tavern is located right at our spawn point area or you can just do /warp Tavern in-game to get teleported at the entrance of the Tavern. Inside the tavern you may find npc's and features such as beer gamble, smithy and custom enchantments ark



AFK Area

You can safely step away from your keyboard without getting kicked off the server. While you’re AFK in the tavern area, you’ll earn between $10 and $100 every 10 minutes!

Safe Zone: No mobs or other players can harm you while you’re here.

Tavern Ambiance: Enjoy the cozy and lively atmosphere of the tavern while you take a break.

Earnings: Collect in-game currency just by being here, ranging from $10 to $100 per 10 minutes.

Relax and Enjoy: Take a break, grab a snack, or just chill while your in-game wallet grows!



Guilds

In Survival, your guild is your team. Many features like guild vaults, guild tier upgrades, guild chats, and more features that you can utilize with your friends. To create your own guild, use the command /guild create [name] [prefix].

Creating a guild costs 10,000 in-game money. If you choose to create your own guild, it is advised that you read the guild command list by using the command /guilds.

If you would like to play with other players, you can invite them to your guild by using the command /guild invite [username] and the invited player must accept the guild invitation by doing /guild accept [guild_name]



Land Claiming

Most players build their bases in the Overworld. You can use the golden shovel to claim a land to protect your base. You can get the free claiming tools by doing /kit claim and if you still don’t know how to claim a land, you may want to check out the video tutorial on how to claim a land using golden shovel at bit.ly/mcgpuser

After settling down and claiming your first land claims, it is recommended that you set a home at that location with /sethome [home_name] so you do not lose it. You can use /homes to view all of your set homes, and /home [home_name] to teleport to a specific one.

A new player have a free one sethome limit, you can get more sethomes by ranking up at [/ranks] (Orange color + Bold ) . If you want to delete your homes, you can use /delhome [home_name] to delete an specific home and set a new one.



Earning Money

As you may be able to tell, Survival is a vast realm with a wide variety of features. This means, you can make money in many different ways, such as:

  • Complete Jobs : /jobs
  • Selling items to the shop :/shop
  • Selling fish to Finn :Finn (NPC at spawn)
  • Selling items at player auctions : /ah
  • Redeeming Daily Rewards : /goose
  • Tavern Gamble : Tavern Gamble
  • Selling currencies or services to other players :/warp ark

While there are many ways to make money, some methods are more profitable than others. Completing Jobs, selling items to the shop, selling items to the player auctions offer better monetary gain compared to other methods.



Player Jobs

One of Survival’s core features is its /jobs feature, which allow players to select and complete a variety of jobs. Everyday, the jobs list in jobs interface have a daily rotation. This means, the jobs interface refresh every 24 hours and the available jobs changes.



Selling Items at Auction

Another way to earn money is by selling items and currencies to other players through the auction house /ah To list an item for sale in the auction house for money, hold the item you want to sell and type /ah sell [price]

Market Place

This is a place where players are allotted plots to build their own shops to sell niche items to players, learning what is trending in the market is the way to go and sell your items to make enough profit to rent your plot!!

Avoid your shop from being demolished if you are inactive or stop maintaining the market plot.

Every month one shop is being chosen to be rewarded under the title of " Merchant of the month "



Player Skills

One of the Server's most exciting Feature which can be accessed by doing /skills, This feature lets you increase your base stats such as increased health , increased damage to Mobs / Players , increased armor durability and many more!!

The Skills levels can be increased by Grinding specific amount materials to gain Experience to level up.

  • Archery - Shoot Mobs or Players with a Bow to earn Archery XP
  • Fighting - Fight Mobs or Players with a weapon to earn Fighting XP
  • Defense - Take damage from Mobs or Players to earn Defense XP
  • Farming - Harvest Crops to earn Farming XP
  • Foraging - Chop Trees to earn Foraging XP
  • Mining - Mine stones or Ores to earn Mining XP
  • Fishing - Catch Fishes to earn Fishing XP
  • Excavation - Dig with a shovel to earn Excavation XP
  • Agility - Move around and take fall Damage to earn Agility XP
  • Alchemy - Brew and splash potions to earn Alchemy XP
  • Enchanting - Enchant and grind items to earn Enchanting XP


Spawn NPC(s)

  • Goose – Rewards

    Goose: Clicking this NPC takes you to the “Rewards” interface, where you can claim your daily, weekly, and monthly free rewards.

  • Bryx – Shop

    Bryx : Clicking this NPC takes you to the “Shop” interface, where you can Buy Ranks and cosmetics.

  • Jobs – Join jobs

    Jobs: Clicking this NPC takes you to the “Jobs” interface, where you can join 3 jobs and complete quests for progress to receive money.

  • Finn – Sell fish

    Finn: Clicking this NPC takes you to the “Fishing” interface, where you can sell your fish for money.

  • Leaderboards – Track progress

    Leaderboards: Clicking this NPC takes you to the “Leaderboard” interface, where you can track your progress and compete to reach the top for Monthly Legends Rewards and earn exclusive badges for milestones.

  • Carpenter – Craft furniture

    Carpenter: Clicking this NPC takes you to the "Furniture" interface, where you can craft unique custom furniture to make your are look more lively.

  • Smithy – Craft armor cosmetics

    Smithy: Clicking this NPC takes you to the "Smithing" interface, where you can craft unique armor cosmetics using materials acquired from dungeons or world drops.

  • Adventurer – Enter dungeons

    Adventurer: Clicking this NPC takes you to the "Dungeon" interface, where you can buy keys using your money to enter into specific dungeons.

  • Bartender – Gamble with beer

    Bartender: Clicking this NPC takes you to the "gambling" interface, where you can test your luck buying various beer bottles and drinking them to get a chance to double your money

  • Guilds – Form or join guilds

    Guilds: Clicking this NPC takes you to the "Guilds" interface, where you can form or join Guilds to your liking.
